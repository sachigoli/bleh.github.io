My Favorite Movies

Cover Name Summary
Stardust Tristan Thorn leaves the non-fiction world for an adventure in faerie land. He needs to collect a fallen star to impress and marry the girl he loves. 10/10 adventure romance and fairytales.
Crazy Rich Asians In a world where education, lineage, and occupation are paramount, the wealthy heir to a modern equivalent to a chinese kingdom brings his girlfriend to meet his family... much to everyone's surprise. 9/10 drama, relatableness, beautiful.
Harry Potter and the Prizoner of Azkaban Harry Potter is being targeted... or is he? A dangerous criminal has escaped and seems to want to seek him out. 7/10 best Harry Potter plot.
The Boy An American woman needing a break from the world gets a job as a nanny for a family in Europe. When she gets there, she realizes that "the boy" she will be taking care of is just... a doll. 6/10 great jumpscares, plot holes, but fun to watch.
Twilight Arizona native Bella Swan travels to Fork, Washington when her mom and new step-dad want to travel the country. Reconnecting with her dad is awkward, but a peculiar family catches her eye and consumes her interest. 8/10 Robert Pattinson is hot.
